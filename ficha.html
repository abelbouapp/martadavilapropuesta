Synopsis & Artistic Vision

"The Silent Overture" reimagines the traditional narrative structure of theater by focusing on the moments of stillness. In a world characterized by constant noise, this production asks: what remains when the dialogue stops?

Using a unique blend of minimalist set design and highly tactile lighting, the performance creates a sensory environment that envelops the audience. Every breath, every subtle movement of the performers is amplified through a bespoke acoustic arrangement.
